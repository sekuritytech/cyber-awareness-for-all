# Cyber Safety Awareness Session

## 🎯 Objective

To educate individuals, parents, and educators about essential cybersecurity practices and safe online behavior.

## 📊 What This Covers

* Why Cyber Safety Matters (with real-world context)
* 5 Golden Rules for staying safe online
* Recognizing common online threats
* Safe Social Media usage (Do’s & Don’ts)
* What to do when something feels wrong
* Password safety fundamentals
* Guidance for parents and educators

## 👥 Target Audience

* Parents
* Children
* Educators
* General users

## ⏱ Duration

45–60 minutes

## 📌 Usage

This presentation can be used for awareness sessions in schools, organizations, and community programs.
